About the College

JNTUH University College of Engineering Jagtial (JNTUH UCEJ) was established in the year 2007 as a constituent college of Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University Hyderabad. The college is recognized by UGC under section 2(f) & 12(B) of the UGC Act, 1956.

JNTUH UCEJ has received THE ENGINEERING EDUCATORS AWARD from the University of Bradford, UK, and Education Matters for its exemplary commitment and impactful positive contribution to the educational sector on February 8, 2014.

The college was accredited by NAAC with A+ in 2022, and the department was accredited by NBA in 2019, re-accredited from 2023 until 2025. The college acquired Autonomous status from 2023 to 2033.

The college is equipped with state-of-the-art laboratories across all departments and has highly qualified faculty, ensuring the best learning experience for students.

Overview of EEE

The Department of Electrical and Electronics Engineering has been outshining in the college since its inception in 2007. It consists of a team of highly experienced and dynamic faculty, dedicated to providing students with quality education.

The EEE Department offers a "UG program" and a "PG program" specializing in "Electrical Power Systems". The department aspires to be a "premier institution in Technical Education", producing "competent electrical engineers" who contribute to the power and electrical sectors through "innovation and expertise".

About the Technical Symposium

"EGNIS" is a "prestigious national-level technical symposium" hosted by the "EEE Department of JNTUH UCEJ". It serves as a "platform for building engineers" to showcase "innovative ideas" and engage with like-minded individuals.

This "exciting event" provides exposure to "emerging technologies and industry practices", including "intense competitions, expert lectures, and hands-on workshops", all designed to enhance technical skills and knowledge.
